Frequently asked questions

What is AlignAI?

AlignAI is a scoring platform that rates AI tools for small businesses based on real community reviews — not vendor marketing or affiliate partnerships. We analyze {{ total_reviews }}+ user experiences across {{ total_categories }} categories to produce honest WORKED, MIXED, or FAILED verdicts on {{ total_tools }}+ tools.

How does AlignAI score AI tools?

AlignAI uses a proprietary 5-Layer Community Intelligence system — not simple star ratings. Our NLP engine analyzes every review for specificity, business context, implementation depth, and sentiment. Reviews are weighted by source credibility, recency, and detail — a 6-month production review counts far more than a vague launch-day comment. The result is a score from 0 to 100 with a community verdict: WORKED (75+), MIXED (50–74), or FAILED (below 50), plus a confidence band. Zero affiliate links. Zero sponsored placements.

Are AI tool review sites trustworthy?

Most aren't. The majority of AI tool review sites are funded by affiliate commissions or sponsored placements — the tools ranked highest are often the ones that pay the most, not the ones that work best. AlignAI was built to solve this by scoring tools exclusively on real community feedback with full methodology transparency.

How is AlignAI different from G2 or Capterra?

G2 and Capterra accept vendor payments for premium placements and sponsored rankings. AlignAI has zero sponsored placements and zero affiliate links. We focus specifically on small businesses and score tools using a proprietary 5-Layer Community Intelligence system and transparent methodology — not vendor-submitted reviews.

Who is AlignAI built for?

AlignAI is built for small business owners, solopreneurs, and lean teams who need to pick the right AI tool without wading through vendor-sponsored review sites. Every score, verdict and review is filtered for small-business context — so you see what works at 1–50 people, not at Fortune 500 scale.

How often are AI tool scores updated?

Tool scores are recomputed continuously as new community reviews arrive, and the homepage stats and top-rated list rebuild daily. When an AI tool ships a major update, model change, or pricing change, we flag affected reviews and re-weight them — so the score reflects the current version of the tool, not the version reviewed 18 months ago.

Can I submit a review for an AI tool?

Yes. Community reviews are the foundation of every score. Any verified small-business user can submit a review, describe their use case and team size, and tag the outcome as WORKED, MIXED or FAILED. Detailed reviews with business context and implementation depth are weighted more heavily than short one-liners.

Does AlignAI take affiliate commissions or sponsored placements?

No. AlignAI has zero affiliate links and zero sponsored placements. Vendors cannot pay to appear, pay to rank higher, or pay to remove reviews. The scoring methodology is public and audit-friendly. This is the core product promise: they rank who pays, we score what works.
